  • ServiceLocator

    The ServiceLocator can be used to register and classes conforming to LocatableService. Use the service locator to prevent creating a lot of singletons for instances you need to use accross the app. Instead, register such classes with the service locator and retrieve them from the service locator as needed. This prevents LocatableService from becoming hard to test.
